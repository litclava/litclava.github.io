What is Literaria Clava?

The club that put the lit in literature.

Literaria Clava, the literary club of G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology and Science was started in the month of February, 2011. The first intra college literary fest, "Lit Out Loud" was also held in the same year. From the year 2013, the literary fest was christened as "LiFe". The multitude of memories that we cherish from Li'Fe and other events have brought us nostalgia with reminders of success and a plethora of experience. Here it is, our journey through the past three years.
