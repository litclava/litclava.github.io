Frequently Asked Quesions

Q) How can I be a member of the club?
A) At the beginning of each academic year we begin the recruitment process which has 2 rounds: Written and an interview. The forms that are shortlisted are called for the interview process. Apart from being a part of the club we also welcome volunteers for events, which, again are chosen through a similar process. All the best! We wish to see you soon!
Q) Any prerequisites to get in?
A) There are no prerequisites to get into the club. An interest in literature is appreciated but you need not a be a bookworm or neither do you have to love to read and write.A keen interested, zealous student is all we look for!
Q) What does Literaria Clava do?
